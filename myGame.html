We used jQuery to let the user pick the date they want to enter for their birthday.
All our pictures are designed by us in Midjourney AI and Photoshop.
We implemented prizes that fall from eliminated enemies with a probability to fall off, and the user can collect them.
We have three kinds of prizes:
1. A banana that increases the monkey's shots by one banana.
2. A timer that increases the time by three seconds.
3. A life that adds one life to the user.

We have four difficulties:
1. Easy - 2 minutes, 3 lives started, 3 banana shots started, weak enemy.
2. Normal - 1.5 minutes, 2 lives started, 2 banana shots started, normal enemy.
3. Hard - 1 minute, 1 life started, 1 banana shot started, hard enemy.
4. Custom - the user can choose the time they want to play with easy difficulty.

in the settings menu, the user can change the volume of the game and the Fire button.

Have fun!
